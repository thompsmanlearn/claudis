## Key decisions

- **Duplicate detection during backfill:** The two n8n lessons were already in ChromaDB using their Supabase UUIDs as doc_ids (older convention — close-session wrote to ChromaDB but forgot to update Supabase chromadb_id column). Created duplicate slug-based entries, detected them via memory_search, deleted the duplicates, and pointed Supabase chromadb_id to the existing UUID-based entries instead. The two RETIRED lessons were genuinely absent from ChromaDB and received new slug-based entries.
- **sync check is surface-only, not auto-fix:** auto-fixing in close-session would hide the root cause. The check surfaces the count; backfill happens deliberately per-session as B-062 procedure.
