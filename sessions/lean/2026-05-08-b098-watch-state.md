## What Was Learned
Novelty detection (URL comparison against existing gather entries) works on first re-run because the watch cycle runs different queries (recent-focused) than the original cycle. Some new URLs are always found in practice. The real "no_change" case would be a very mature, slow-moving topic — reasonable behavior.
