# /sessions/monthly

Monthly development snapshots. Written during a scheduled end-of-month session.

**Naming:** `YYYY-MM.md`

**Purpose:** Richer than on-demand `/gh_report`. Written with external readability as primary goal — the artifact a future public reader would find most useful. Covers the month's arc: what was built, how priorities shifted, what changed about Claudis's self-understanding.

**Distinction from `/sessions/`:** Daily sessions capture what happened. Monthly snapshots synthesize what it meant.
